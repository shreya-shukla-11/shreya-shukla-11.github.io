Mining Data Dumps

For this reviews project of restaurants, the basic thing a person can think to digest such large data is to summarize it, but if you are creative enough, you can get gold standard insights from such large chunks of data. We were able to get USPs of restaurants that were later used to enhance their discoverability on search too. In this project, I was exposed to good product management and crazy creativity and I learned how you should use your creativity to get crazy insights from any large dumps of data.
